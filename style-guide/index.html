Design System

The Gambito style guide.

The system behind the site — colour, type, components, motion and voice. Built to feel like a sophisticated presence in a black classy dress: intimidatingly composed, then warm and human the moment you engage.

Brand direction

The through-line for every decision.

The gambit

A gambit is a confident opening move — risking a small piece for a decisive advantage. Everything ladders back to helping founders move from hesitation to action.

Composed, then warm

Deep, restrained, expensive at first glance. Approachable, empathetic and jargon-free the moment you interact. Never cold, never loud.

Motion is the medium

The beauty comes from movement — a scroll-scrubbed cinematic backdrop and considered, physics-led transitions rather than decoration.

One bold note

A single coral accent, used like lipstick — sparingly, deliberately, and only where it earns attention.

Motion

Every movement is scroll-driven, eased and deliberate.

01

Scrubbed backdrop

A page-wide video scrubs to scroll position — the frame advances as you move, never plays on its own.

02

Scene camera

The backdrop pans and zooms scene by scene on a single continuous timeline, settling before the contact section.

03

Word-scrub reveals

Copy lights up word by word as it enters view, tying reading pace to scroll.

04

The easing

Primary curve is cubic-bezier(0.19, 1, 0.22, 1) — a quick, confident settle.

Voice & tone

Confident, warm, and completely free of jargon.

Say it plainly

If you wouldn't say it at dinner, don't write it. Explain, never posture.

Short, then sharp

Lead with a plain line, land with a pointed one. "Launch is move one."

Speak to the founder

Empathetic and direct. We name the hesitation, then offer the move.

Chess, lightly

The gambit metaphor is a seasoning, not the whole meal — openings, moves, the board.
